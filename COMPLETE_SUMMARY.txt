## 🔮 What Happens When You Press H

```
1. Your press H key
2. hint_toggle flips on
3. If first time: spawn background thread
4. Thread calls: get_best_move_and_score()
5. Calculates best move & position score
6. Main thread checks if done each frame
7. When ready: highlights appear on board
8. Evaluation bar updates with score
9. Press H again: highlights disappear
```

**Total time**: ~0.3-0.5 seconds
**Game time**: 0% freeze (fully responsive)
